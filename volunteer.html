    • Become a Volunteer

    Join our team and help us make a difference. Volunteering with MedicalCare is a rewarding experience where you can contribute to your community.

    Medical Volunteer

    As a Medical Volunteer, you will work closely with our medical team to assist with patient care and perform various essential tasks that support the healthcare process. Your responsibilities may include preparing patients for examinations, assisting with basic medical procedures, monitoring patient vital signs, and ensuring that medical supplies are adequately stocked. This role provides a hands-on experience in a healthcare setting, allowing you to make a direct impact on patient well-being while gaining valuable experience in the medical field.

    Community Outreach

    The Community Outreach role is essential for spreading awareness and educating the public about critical health issues and available services. Volunteers in this role will engage directly with community members through various outreach activities, including organizing health fairs, giving presentations on health topics, and distributing educational materials. This role is ideal for those who are passionate about public health and enjoy working with diverse groups of people to promote healthier lifestyles and improve community well-being.

    Administrative Support

    Volunteers in the Administrative Support role provide crucial assistance to our office staff, helping to ensure that the organization's day-to-day operations run smoothly. This role involves a variety of administrative tasks, such as managing records, answering phones, organizing files, and assisting with scheduling appointments. It's an excellent opportunity for individuals who are detail-oriented and interested in learning more about the administrative side of healthcare or nonprofit work.
